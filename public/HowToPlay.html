How to Play "Save The Robbin"

  • **Movement:** Use **Arrow Keys** (Desktop) or **On-Screen Controls** (Mobile) to move "Robbin".
  • **Goal:** Navigate the network maze to collect all data packets and advance levels.
  • **Data Packets (Blue):** Collect them to score points (+10 per packet).
  • **Viruses (Red):** These are cyber threats. Avoid them or you will lose a life!
  • **Firewall Boost (Purple):** Collect this power-up. When active, you can defeat viruses and earn bonus points. The effect is temporary.
  • **Level Up:** Reach the score threshold (e.g., 250 points) to advance to the next level. Viruses become faster and smarter with each level!
